My Hobbies

Hiking

I love to go hiking. As a kid, I was a Boy Scout, which instilled a lifelong love of the outdoors. I have hiked all throughout Florida, many miles of the Appalachian Trail, in the desert of Nevada, and remote parts of Canada.

Poker

I love to play poker. I have made many trips to Las Vegas, and competed in the World Series of Poker. I started playing poker in college after my roommates and I saw the movie "Rounders".

Theme Parks

I love roller coasters. I have annual passes to Universal Studios and Walt Disney World. I look forward all year long to Halloween Horror Nights.

Karaoke

I'm a terrible singer. I don't let that stop me from singing karaoke after a few beers. My friends and I love to sing karaoke, and compete in bar trivia.
